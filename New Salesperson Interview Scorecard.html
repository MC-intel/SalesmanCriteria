Scoring Guide

5 = Exceptional - Clearly demonstrates mastery; top 10% of candidates

4 = Strong - Above average; shows solid evidence of this trait

3 = Adequate - Meets basic requirements; average performance

2 = Below Average - Some concerns; needs development

1 = Poor - Significant deficiency; major concern

Hiring Recommendation Guidelines:
